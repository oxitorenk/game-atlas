## Perfect
- I loved the art design. It felt like I was making pizza inside a hand-drawn fairy tale book.

## Good
- The story progression and my interactions with the characters really hooked me.
- The sound effects are incredibly satisfying.
- The [[#Garden]] mechanics were a great breathing point for me.

## Poor
- The main gameplay loop started to bore me after a while. At some point, I kept playing just because I was curious about what would happen in the story.
- The upgrades felt a bit insufficient to me. They generally just serve to automate the tasks I do and are not very creative.

## Terrible
- I struggled a lot with User Experience (UX); it's full of choices that feel way behind today's standards and outdated.
	- For example, once I pick up the pizza cutter, there's no turning back; I have to finish that operation. Not being able to cancel even if I do something wrong stressed me out a lot.
- Mobile game behaviors flow from every part of the PC version; in-game purchases (IAP) appearing at every corner ruined my enjoyment by giving a "free game" feeling.
- I experienced a complete technical fiasco. Because of cloud save problems, the effort I put in for hours was deleted several times. The game becoming unplayable in this way was the last straw for me and I had to return it.
